About

My name is Qasim Alyas but most people call me kas, it’s simpler, right?

I currently work for Abacus e-Media as a front-end developer and am passionate about HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Accessibility and more.

This site is mainly a playground for me to do some experimental stuff with HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript so it might not look the best in a browser like Internet Explorer. I could have spent some extra time making it work but I want to show off the capabilities of these new technologies. It will look much better in modern browsers like, Safari, FireFox or Chrome. These are all faster and free!
